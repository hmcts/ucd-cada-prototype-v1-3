ApplicationsUpload your application

Upload your application to the case. If you are uploading a paper copy of the application, make sure this has been scanned in clearly, and saved in a suitable file format such as PDF.

Files must be saved as a JPG, BMP, PNG, TIF, PDF, DOC or DOCX to be added to the case.

Take a picture of a document on your phone and upload it
  1. Place your document on a flat service in a well-lit room. Use a flash if you need to.
  2. Take a picture of the whole document. You should be able to see its edges.
  3. Check you can read all the writing, including the handwriting.
  4. Email or send the photo or scan to the device you are using now.
  5. Upload it here.
